You support the Assignment 2 customer review for Team 40. The customer is Danila Danko, the senior student who built the original Voice Word Jumper demo.

## Before the meeting, prepare

- an agenda covering user stories, MoSCoW priorities, and the initial proposed MVP v1 scope
- approval questions phrased so the customer can explicitly approve the user stories, the priorities, and the MVP v1 scope
- a recording permission script, including consent to share a sanitized English transcript privately with instructors
- prototype feedback questions about the home, game selection, main game (microphone interaction), and results screens

## During and after the meeting, help write

- customer-meeting-summary.md
- customer-meeting-transcript.md if publication is permitted
- customer-meeting-notes.md if recording or private sharing is refused

The summary must include:
- date
- participants or roles
- artifacts demonstrated
- discussion points
- decisions
- action points
- risks
- feedback
- customer approvals (user stories, priorities, MVP v1 scope, and written consent to the public MIT-licensed development model)
- resulting changes

Remove PII and confidential information. Keep everything in English.
